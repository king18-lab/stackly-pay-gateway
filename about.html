FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are some of the most common questions about Stackly.

How secure is Stackly for processing payments?

Stackly uses bank-level 256-bit encryption, PCI DSS Level 1 compliance, and advanced fraud detection systems to ensure every transaction is completely secure.

What are the transaction fees?

Our pricing is transparent and competitive. We offer 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction for most cards, with volume discounts available for high-volume businesses.

How long does it take to get paid?

Funds are typically available in your bank account within 1-2 business days. We also offer instant payouts for qualifying merchants.

Do you support international payments?

Yes! Stackly supports payments in 135+ currencies and can process transactions from customers in over 190 countries worldwide.

What kind of support do you offer?

We provide 24/7 customer support via live chat, email, and phone. Plus, we have extensive documentation and a dedicated account manager for enterprise clients.
